How We Make Money

We believe in transparency. Here's exactly how MultiMatch generates revenue — and how it affects your experience.

🔗

Affiliate Commissions

When you click on a product link and make a purchase at a retailer, we may earn a small commission from that retailer — at no extra cost to you. The price you pay is the same whether you use our link or go directly to the retailer.

We participate in affiliate programs including CJ Affiliate, Rakuten Advertising, Amazon Associates, eBay Partner Network, and others.

Important: Affiliate commissions do not influence our product rankings. We always show you the genuinely best deal based on price, rating, and delivery speed — not based on which retailer pays the highest commission.

Our Ranking Philosophy

Search results are ranked by a combination of:

  • Price — lower prices rank higher
  • Rating — higher customer ratings rank higher
  • Delivery speed — faster delivery ranks higher
  • Availability — in-stock items appear before out-of-stock

Commission rates vary by retailer and product category. A retailer paying a higher commission does not get preferential placement. Our "Best Match" algorithm is designed to serve you, not retailers.

Future Revenue Plans

As MultiMatch grows, we may introduce additional revenue streams:

⭐

Premium Tier (Future)

An optional paid subscription with advanced features like unlimited price alerts, detailed spend analytics, and ad-free experience. The core search and order tracking features will always remain free.

📊

Aggregated Insights (Future)

Anonymized shopping trend data may be licensed to retailers and brands. This data never includes individual user information — only aggregate patterns like "headphones are searched 40% more in November."
